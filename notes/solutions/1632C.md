# [1632C](https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/1632/C)

## Problem 
Given `a` and `b`. (`a, b <= 1e6`)
Apply 3 types of op: 
1. `a += 1`
2. `b += 1`
3. `a |= b`

Min `nOps` to make `a = b`

## Approach 

***Obs.*** Use op 3 at most once at the last moment 

***Approach 1.***
Let `a'` and `b'` are the values of `a` and `b` after some operation.

The result is `(a' - a) + (b' - b) + (a'|b' - b') + 1` 
or `a' + a'|b' + 1 - a - b`

For each `a'` minimize `a'|b'`

***Obs.*** Either use op 1 or op 2 

***Approach 2.***
Iterate from `a` to `b` to find `a'`
Iterate from `b` to `b+a` to find `b'`
