# 1108B Codeforces

## Problem 
[Link](https://codeforces.com/problemset/problem/1455/B)

## Approach

***Observation***
1. Op1 increase fast and op2 decrease small
2. $\exists! \ n$ such that: $\frac{n^2+n}{2} \ge x \ge \frac{n^2-n}{2}$
3. $\frac{n^2+n}{2} - x \le n$ (proved by 2)

## Solution 
Find minimum `n` - number of op1 to "reach" the `x` and then use op2 wisely to adjust to `x`
